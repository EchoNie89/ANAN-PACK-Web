We may collect personal and business information when you visit our website, contact us, submit an inquiry, request a quotation, upload files, or communicate with our team.

Information you provide directly may include:

- Name
- Company name
- Job title
- Email address
- Phone number or WhatsApp number
- Country or region
- Product requirements
- Project details
- Quantity requirements
- Delivery requirements
- Uploaded files, artwork, logos, packaging references, technical documents, or design files
- Any other information you choose to provide

Information collected automatically may include:

- IP address
- Browser type
- Device type
- Pages visited
- Time spent on the website
- Referring website or traffic source
- General location data
- Cookie and analytics data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for business and service purposes, including to:

- Respond to your inquiries
- Prepare quotations
- Understand your packaging and trim requirements
- Communicate about product development, samples, production, quality control, and delivery
- Provide sourcing and supply chain coordination services
- Review artwork, logos, packaging references, and technical files
- Coordinate with selected supplier partners or production partners
- Arrange samples, production, inspection, packing, and shipment
- Improve our website, services, and user experience
- Send relevant business communications, where permitted
- Maintain internal business records
- Protect our website, systems, and business operations
- Comply with applicable legal or regulatory requirements

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve browsing experience, understand website traffic, and analyze visitor behavior.

We may use analytics tools, such as Google Analytics or similar services, to collect aggregated website usage data. These tools help us understand how visitors find and use our website.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some website functions may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

## 5. How We Share Information

We may share your information only when necessary for business purposes, including with:

- Internal team members
- Selected supplier partners
- Production partners
- Logistics providers
- Website hosting providers
- Email service providers
- Analytics service providers
- Professional advisors, where necessary
- Authorities or regulators, if required by law

We only share information that is necessary for the relevant purpose, such as preparing quotations, developing samples, evaluating materials, checking production feasibility, coordinating production, conducting quality checks, or arranging shipment.

We do not share your confidential business information with unrelated third parties.

## 6. Supplier and Production Coordination

As a one-stop packaging and trims supply chain service provider, we may share relevant project information with selected supplier partners or production partners.

This may include:

- Product specifications
- Material requirements
- Quantity information
- Artwork files
- Logos
- Packaging references
- Technical drawings
- Sample requirements
- Production requirements
- Delivery schedules

This information is used only to evaluate materials, pricing, sampling, production feasibility, quality requirements, and delivery arrangements.

We do not allow unrelated third parties to use your project information for their own marketing or unrelated business purposes.

## 7. Uploaded Files, Artwork, and Logos

If you upload or send us artwork, logos, packaging designs, reference images, technical documents, or other project files, we will use those files only for the purpose of responding to your inquiry, preparing quotations, developing samples, coordinating production, or providing related services.

Please do not upload confidential files unless they are necessary for your inquiry or quotation request.

If your files contain third-party trademarks, logos, artwork, or designs, you are responsible for ensuring that you have the right to use and provide those materials to us.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Because we work with international clients, suppliers, service providers, and production partners, your information may be processed or transferred across borders, including to China or other countries where our team, service providers, or supply chain partners operate.

We take reasonable steps to protect your information when it is transferred internationally.

## 9. Data Retention

We keep personal and project information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

This may include inquiry follow-up, quotation records, project management, sample development, production coordination, customer service, legal obligations, dispute handling, and business recordkeeping.

If you would like us to delete your personal information, you may contact us at [services@ananpack.com](mailto:services@ananpack.com).

## 10. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
